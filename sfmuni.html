Ayla Hoffman. The Reality of Cut Flowers, 2023. Indesign, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop.The featured type studies were developed in Fall of 2023. The content and compositions contend with a diversity of subject matters, mediums, typographic arrangements, hierarchy of information, and underlying text/grid structures. This series serves to catalogue a foundational understanding of how text and composition come together to create meaning and interest. These type studies also establish an understanding of typographic experimentation, functionality, and working within visual constraints/systems.
